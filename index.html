About Us

Who We Are

Apple Hostel is a premier hostel dedicated to providing students with a comfortable, and affordable living environment. Our spaces are designed to balance study, rest, and social connection, offering a true home-away-from-home experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make hostel living stress free and enjoyable by combining affordability with quality. We aim to suppport residents with the facilities and services they need to ficus on their studies, build friendships, and enjoy their time with peace of mind.

Why Choose Us

  • 24/7 security, steady power, and reliable water supply
  • Affordable pricing with services that add real value
  • A supportive environment that feels like home
